Current centralized funding agencies
cannot meet the demands of
biomechanical research
• Most proposals (>90%) are not funded or triaged
• Time-consuming (up to several years from application
to funding)
• Lack of transparency in review and spending
• Price overhead (administrative cost)
• Difficult to renew
Each of these issues may be solved
by decentralized funding (DEFI)
• Enable Crowdfunding
• Need-based
• Funds may be available in real-time
• Minimized overhead
• Blockchain ensuring transparency and unaltered funding spending
• Voting
• Sustained research support
